About The Owner

Joel Raj

My job is to make someone look and feel good about them selves using the skills that I have. I’m very honest and open with my clients. I love what I do and that’s why I’m so lucky in my profession. About The Business

I’ve had my business since 2016 now and I’m always looking for new ways to find clients. I’ve always loved experimenting cuts and changing everyone’s style to make them happy. It’s always about the client for me. They always come first. The chair tells a million stories. I’m always happy to have a chat and always interested to meet new people and talk about their hobbies.
